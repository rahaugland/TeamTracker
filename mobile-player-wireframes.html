Design Notes

Quick RSVP Focus

Every event shows immediate RSVP status or quick action buttons. Players can respond with a single tap using checkmark/X buttons, reducing friction for schedule management.

Gamified Stats

FIFA-style rating cards make performance tracking engaging and familiar. Overall rating, skill breakdowns, and trend indicators create a game-like progression system that motivates improvement.

Bottom Navigation

Thumb-optimized tab bar keeps primary navigation always accessible. Active states use the club red color with subtle background pills for clear visual feedback.

Streak Mechanics

Attendance and practice streaks with emoji icons and prominent counters encourage consistency. The "Best!" callout adds positive reinforcement for maintaining good habits.

Timeline Feedback

Coach feedback displayed as a vertical timeline with visual indicators for highlighted entries. Creates a progression narrative and recognizes standout performances.

Self-Reflection

Players can rate their own sessions and submit reflections. The gold-accented assessment card stands out while maintaining the overall dark sports aesthetic.

Contextual Badges

Event type badges (Practice, Match, Tournament) use semantic colors from the design system. Teal for practice, red for matches, gold for special events.

Progressive Disclosure

Home screen shows next event hero card with full details, while upcoming list shows compact versions. Players get critical info at a glance without overwhelming the interface.
