Components

Cards

Consistent card patterns used across both views. Club accent stripe on top, subtle borders, and hover elevation.

Info Card Default

Standard card with accent stripe, header with metadata, and body content. Used for schedule items, announcements, and stat summaries.

Teal Accent App Brand

When content is app-level (not club-specific), the teal accent is used instead of club colors. Settings, onboarding, cross-club features.

Highlighted Card

Full club-color background for high-priority items: match day alerts, critical announcements, call-to-action prompts.

Onboarding

First Impressions

Two paths, both fast. Coaches create the club and invite players. Players join via code, set up a minimal profile, and land on their team. No fake gamification until real data exists.
